NUVEI | DESIGN & MOTION

Every payment,
everywhere

Nuvei is a global fintech company that builds the infrastructure for every payment, everywhere. With locations all over the world, Nuvei is one system connecting markets, methods, and currencies to help businesses move faster and grow with confidence.

Nuvei brought in an external agency to refresh their visual identity and modernise the brand.

My challenge was taking that baseline and rolling it out across the Australian and New Zealand regions while also assisting the global teams. My day-to-day responsibilities included maintaining and managing the brand across Australia and New Zealand, while creating collateral across print, motion, social media, events, digital campaigns, and more. Alongside this, I supported the global teams with brand collateral for regions including Canada, Japan, Italy, France, and Spain, ensuring the new visual identity was consistently applied across a wide range of touchpoints.
