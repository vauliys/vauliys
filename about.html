Victoria Pritchard (vauliys) is a Melbourne-based Digital Designer, specialising in Digital Illustrations, 2D Motion Graphics, Print and Digital Medias.

With 6 years of study under her belt, she has attained a Bachelor's Degree in Design (Animation and Interactive Media) from RMIT University and a Certificate III (Screen and Media) from Kangin Institue. Victoria has Interned with the Melbourne Museum under the Creative Programs, granted to her following the participation in the 2018 Top Designs Exhibition showcasing her original Animated Short "Daisy and Poh".

She currently works in the Fintech Industry, under the employement of Till Payments enforcing brand identity across print and video, while bringing new life and movement to virtual design.

You can learn more about Victoria on her Linkedin, IMDB or Toon Boom Ambassador Profile.
