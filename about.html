Who is Victoria?

Victoria Pritchard (vauliys) is a Melbourne-based Digital Designer, specialising in Motion Graphics and Digital Illustrations.

She currently works in the Fintech Industry, under the employement of Till Payments enforcing brand identity across print and video, while bringing new life and movement to virtual design.

Previously, Victoria has produced projects for reputable brands and organisations (Logitech, Australian Red Cross and Volvo).

Her experiences have not only shown her dedication to her craft but has also presented the opportunity to work alongside Rick and Morty creators in the Hulu Animated Series "Solar Opposites".
