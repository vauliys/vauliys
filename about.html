Kindergarden, 2004

Meet the artist.

Victoria spent majority of her childhood drawing, developing throughout the years. Commencing her first year of high-school, Victoria was granted her own personal laptop, an Acer B115 Travelmate - this opened doors for her to the world of digital media. Victoria spent 6 years creating illustrations with the trackpad of her laptop, and uploading speedpaints to Youtube. In 2016 Channel Frederator, an entertainment network , signed her YouTube channel.

Country Girl Management, Australia, 2018

Igniting her passion for animation.

From 2016 to 2017, Victoria studied a VET Certificate III in Screen and Media at Kanging Institute while completing her VCE studies in high-school. Following secondary education, Victoria Interned with the Melbourne Museum and was selected to exhbit her graduate film at the Top Designs Exhibition in 2018. Victoria continued her studies until 2021, studying a Bachelor of Design specialising in Animation and Interactive Media. In her spare time Victoria volunteered as a Content Creator for the Australian Red Cross social pages.

Himeji Castle 姫路城, Himeji, Japan, 2023

Where is she now?

Victoria currently works as a full-time Digital Designer for Fintech company, Till Payments. On the side she takes on additional freelance projects for comapnies including Logitech and XPPEN while posting her artwork and brand collations on social platforms.
