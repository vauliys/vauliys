Lashes by Rhi

For the love of beauty.

A beauty salon founded in Delacombe, Victoria, thrives on providing high-quality cosmetic and personal care services, varying from eyelash extensions to body waxing.

Lashes by Rhi aim to supply only the best quality products and deliver the up-most care to clients. As an increase in clientele grows and demands for service are at an all-time high, Lashes by Rhi must evolve too.

From initial concepts of logos and branding based on the founder's eye shape, it has evolved into a simplistic design still representing the original concept, becoming more readable on micro scales and easy to identify. Features of floral patterns and line graphics appear throughout the brand showcasing the usage of organic ingredients and Lashes By Rhi's missionary statement to be eco-friendly.

The lilac tones complimenting the branding represent the youthfulness and welcoming environment Lashes by Rhi aims to provide. Lilac symbolizes compassion and kindness, creating a relaxed and soothing service for the clients.
