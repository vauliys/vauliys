Digital Sketchbook - a collection of Vauliys sketches.

Vauliys believes that not every artwork requires a complete rendering and, as she matures as an artist, she has begun to unveil the sketches that are present behind her finalized illustrations or those that remain hidden and stored on her computer. Vauliys truly savours the process of sketching, and to give a soft and textured visual effect, she employs her own custom brush, called "Sketch Smooth."
