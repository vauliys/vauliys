How do I enquire for an Illustrative Commission?

How much does an Illustrative Commission cost?

Please refer to the pricing guide for my illustrative commissions. It is important to note that the prices are just a guide, as there are several factors that could influence the final cost, such as level of detail, number of characters, inclusion of props.

What is the typical process for an Illustrative Commission?

The typical process for an illustrative commission with me involves a discussion between both parties, where we go in depth about your commission concept, pricing, and expectations.

Once an agreement is reached and payment/ deposit is made, I’ll begin to work on the project by presenting an initial sketch and concept, which is refined until a final high-res product is created and delivered.

At every stage I will provide a low-res image where you are given the opportunity to provide feedback and make a change if requested. Your commission includes up to 3 revisions and for every extra revision there includes an additional fee.

How long does an illustrative commission usually take?

The time required for an illustrative commission varies depending on factors like the detail of the piece, my workload, and your timeline.

Typically, illustrative commissions can take anywhere from a few weeks to a month.

If you have a rush deadline and request the final deliverable within a week there is an additional fee.

What I will/won't draw.

I am pretty open to a wide variety of concepts including NSFW, Fanart, Gore.

I will however reject any concepts including Sexualisation of Minors, Political or Religious, Offensive Material, or drawing in another style.

For any Furry commissions, a small discount will be taken off your final cost as it’s not something I specialise in but is a concept I’d like to improve in.

Can I get a refund?

Unfortunately, once a commission has started a refund will not be granted.

If there are any issues with the final artwork or product delivered, I am always open to discussing potential solutions to ensure your satisfaction but it is encouraged to state your feedback during the feedback sessions.

Please note that all sales are final and refunds will only be granted under certain circumstances (ie, I am unable to perform, get sick etc).

Can I use the illustration commercially?

This depends on the licensing agreement between you and myself.

Typically, commercial use is not allowed, but you must confirm the licensing specifics with me before you can use the artwork for commercial purposes. Commercial usage includes an additional fee.
