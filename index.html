Howdy hey, I'm Victoria, also known as Vauliys, a multi-talented artist, designer, and animator based in the Australia. My love for technology and art provides a fresh burst of energy and colour to the creative scene, with a big dose of playful and imaginative vibes.

Starting my career freelancing for Logitech, Australian Redcross and Solar Opposites, I have transitioned to a full-time Digital Designer in the Fintech Industry under Till Payments, combining my passions of technology with art. Apart from creating amazing digital paintings, graphics, and illustrations.

I enjoy sharing my insights into my creative process and collaborating with other artists. I consistently inspire and provide tips for other aspiring artists who seek to advance and hone their craft. My art has been featured in several publications, and my social media presence continues to grow, with thousands of loyal fans following my journey. My works have also been featured in art shows, galleries, and exhibitions.
