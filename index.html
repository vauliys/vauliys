Nice to meet you :)

Howdy hey, I'm Victoria, also known as Vauliys, a multi-talented artist, designer, and animator based in Australia.

Starting my career freelancing for Logitech, Australian Redcross and Solar Opposites, I have transitioned to a full-time Digital Designer in the Fintech Industry under Till Payments, combining my passions of technology with art.

My art has been featured in several publications, and my social media presence continues to grow, with over 40K+ collective fans following my journey.
