Top Designs Exhibition @ The Melboune Museum.

Top Designs showcases a selection of Victoria's brightest VCE design students, offering a glimpse into the future of contemporary Australian design. Vauliys was chosen to showcase her animated short film Daisy and Poh in the VCE Creative and Digital Media Category at the Top Designs Exhibition during her post-graduation year in 2018. Out of the exhibitors, she was selected along with four others to take part in the Creative Internship Program held at the Melbourne Museum, which commenced on the opening day of the exhibition.
